BRAZIL

Brazil is the largest country in South America and the fifth largest nation in the world. It forms an enormous triangle on the eastern side of the continent with a 4,500-mile (7,400-kilometer) coastline along the Atlantic Ocean. It has borders with every South American country except Chile and Ecuador. It also displays beautiful land that everyone has heard of like the amazon river and the amazon rainforest, Brazil also has amazing culture that blends with other indigenous cultures.

WHY YOU SHOULD VISIT

Carnival is just another reason to travel to Brazil. It is the most well-known national holiday in the nation and is celebrated all day and all night for around a week. Rio has raised the standard for carnival, is the largest and most well-known celebration in the nation (and undoubtedly the world), and welcomes over half a million foreign visitors annually. Extensive parades featuring dancers, unique costumes, samba music, and more are part of Rio's massive street festival. It's a fantastic time to travel to the country and experience Brazilian culture at its peak if you enjoy partying. Sounds like my kind of party! People party all day long until the sun comes up.
